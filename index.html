    Individuals with Inattentive ADHD and reading problems often struggle with focus retention, which manifests as poor reading skills. Our website aims to alleviate their struggles with a holistic approach. By summarizing the story, Clarithink makes it easier for children to grasp key ideas without overwhelming them. Furthermore, the application strategically generates images related to the text to further emphasize the story, fostering increased interest and providing a new perspective for the children. Additionally, Clarithink includes pop-up questions to encourage children to continue reading, thus increasing their awareness of their comprehension level.

    Certainly, the tools work for all ages. However, the content is designed for young children. The books shown on the website are short stories dedicated mostly for children that have reading problems and difficulty retaining focus.

    Strong reading skills and comprehension significantly impact both academic and professional performance, serving as essential tools for students to effectively grasp crucial information and instructions necessary for their educational endeavors. Individuals with reading disabilities and inattentive ADHD often struggle with underlying issues, which manifest as poor reading skills, leading to inadequate academic or work performance. Assistive tools play a pivotal role in supporting individuals with reading disabilities and inattentive ADHD, alleviating their challenges while harnessing their unique strengths and interests to their advantage."

    About Us

    Here at ClariThink, we understand the challenges faced by children with ADHD and reading problems, and we are dedicated to sparking joy in reading for them. Through our innovative approach, we reimagine classic children's stories into fun and concise versions, making reading engaging and accessible for all.


    Our Mission

    We strive to make reading fun and digestible for every children, especially those who struggle with reading comprehension and ADHD, by simplifying the stories and providing visual aids.
    Acknowledging the stereotypes and research gaps surrounding ADHD, our team is committed to developing more tools to enhance their educational journey while advocating for greater awareness.
